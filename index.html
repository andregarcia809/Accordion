Cool facts about JavaScript

When was JavaScript created?

+

JavaScript, not to be confused with java, was created in 10 days in May 1995.

JavaScrip was developed under the name Mocha.

JavaScrip was created to act as a "glue language" that was easy to use by Web designers and part-time programmers to assemble components such as images and plugins, where the code could be written directly in the Web page markup.

Who created JavaScrip?

+

Brendan Eich.

Brendan Eich earned his Master's degree from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 1985 and later worked at Silicon Graphics for seven years.
